Baked Salmon

Description

Fresh, flaky and juicy salmon. Perfect dinner for two.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets - 6 ounces each
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 medium lemon
  • fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and grease a large baking pan. Place salmon fillets on baking sheet and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stir together olive oil, minced garlic, italian seasoning, and juice from 1/2 of lemon.Spoon over salmon and brush on all sides. Thinly slice remaining 1/2 of lemon and top each fillet with a slice of lemon.
  3. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes until samlmon is flaky when pulled apart.
  4. Garnish with parsley and serve.
