Lasagna

Description

Mama Mia's famous lasagna. Perfect for any occasion.

In no time you'll have a splendid dish the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles uncooked
  • 1/2 pound of ground beef
  • 2 jars of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 garlic cloves minced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large skillet, cook beef till browned. Drain fat.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, paste, and spices. Simmer uncovered for 5 minutes.
  4. In casserole dish add place noodles and add sauce on top. Top with cheese.
  5. Place in microwave for 45 minutes.
  6. Remove dish and let sit for 15 minutes before cutting.
