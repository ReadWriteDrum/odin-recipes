Tacos

Description

Take a trip the Mexico without leaving the house.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • corn tortillas
  • lettuce finely chopped
  • shredded Mexican cheese
  • Tomatoes chopped

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain fat.
  2. Add spices, tomato paste, and water. Stir to combine and cook over medium low heat until sauce thickens.
  3. Serve warm on tortillas. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
